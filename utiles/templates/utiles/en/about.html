About Us

Welcome to Nakiese.com, your gateway to African life!

Nakiese is more than just a booking site. The term “Nakiese” comes from Kikongo, a language spoken in several regions of Central Africa, and means “to enjoy or be in joy.” Composed of the words “Na” (in) and “Kiese” (joy, happiness, satisfaction), Nakiese embodies the essence of African life: a life where comfort, satisfaction, and joy are within reach.

Our Mission

At Nakiese, we believe in the hidden beauty of Africa. Our mission is to reveal the continent’s treasures, from authentic accommodations to unique culinary experiences, and make them accessible to all. We are determined to transform the way the world discovers and experiences Africa.

What We Do?

Nakiese.com is an online platform dedicated to booking accommodation, restaurants, and other essential services for an authentic experience in Africa. Our goal is to facilitate the discovery of the cultural, natural and human riches of the continent, by connecting travelers to local establishments.

Whether you're looking for a charming hotel, a quaint guesthouse, or a restaurant that serves local specialties, Nakiese makes it easy to find and book.
